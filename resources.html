  2. Middleschool: Grade 2

  3. Middleschool: Grade 3

    Information and Communication Technologies / ICT

    This was my favourite time in middleschool I ever had. I got a really nice class which I've really appreciated and I've had really good teachers there, willing to help, see you succeed. At the end Elecro- mechanica didn't save the day. We didn't see any Mechanics. Electricity was a major subject though. I am really lucky I did Electro-mechanica before ICT because I noticed some people dropping out after the first year because it was too difficult, or not their taste.

    This was also the first year I got an integrated project. It was fun, not really the thing I would personally do but I liked working on it. At this point though, my vision of the future became vague a few times. First I wanted to continue in Software and become a gamedeveloper, but I've kind of out-grown that by the time I started third grade. So then I thought about AI, that's a really big interest of mine, thing is though, I couldn's see myself analyzing data days on end. So I opted out of that. Lastly Networking seemed like a nice thing to do. Installing servers, configuring switches, debugging a network, and those sort of things. I seem to be really interested in those kind of things and I scored good points on them. My teacher told me to become Industrial Engeneer and thats when I decided that I would do that in University.

    *As of writing this I am still in middleschool.
